Overview of the Major Introductory Courses Principles of Economics (ECON 1101) Statistics (ECON 1151) Intermediate Courses Microeconomic Theory (ECON 2201 or ECON 2203) Macroeconomic Theory (ECON 2202 or ECON 2204) Econometric Methods (ECON 2228) Both intermediate theory courses and Econometrics should be completed by the end of the sophomore year. Those who start the major late should have these completed by first semester junior year. Elective Courses six electives chosen from ECON 2200-ECON 4497. At least four of these SIX courses must be upper lever 3000+ elective courses (except ECON 3336), ECON 4497.
