Welcome!

This website was created to make it easier for Boston College undergraduate students to identify what courses they need to take. The current method of viewing course offerings organizes classes by department, often leaving confusion regarding which classes will count towards a certain major's requirements. Boston College Course Rolodex presents courses by major so students can better keep track of their progress, as well as plan their future schedules. In order to find your major, click on the Boston College undergraduate school in which the major is a part of in the navigation bar. From there, you will be able to chose from the top three most popular majors in each school, and view the required courses and times offered. Please click here to complete our feedback form!
